Experience Calculator

Enter the values of a and b to calculate the experience points required to reach level 7.9
Formula is a*(log(level)^b), where level is the float representaion of the grades and ranks for example; 3.4 = 3rd grade with 4th rank





Levels are split into "grades" and "ranks"
Grades are the whole number part of the level, and ranks are the decimal part of the level.
Grades are numbers 1 to 7.
ranks are numbers 1 to 9, when the rank reaches 9 the grade increases and the rank resets back to 1.
